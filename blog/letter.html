This is an automated upload in the event that it isn't disabled. At the time of writing this, it is the 15th of May. If you are reading this, then I am most likely dead.


Writing letters like this one feels familiar to me, it shouldn't. Not enough apologies will ever be enough for what I'm putting everyone through right now, and I will not forgive myself for it, but I hope that you will find it in you to forgive me, eventually. Some of you will understand why I did this, and others will never, I do not blame you. I am deeply sorry for everyone who has invested their time, efforts, and money in me, I wish that I could repay you all.


I am also sorry that I did not talk to anyone, talking has not been helping me much at all for quite some years now and it would not have helped. Please don't blame yourself, it was nobody's fault but mine. I appreciate the help that I got and I wish the circumstances were better.


I understand that grief is the price you pay for love and I love you all. However, please do not feel sad for me, I want this, and I need this, and I am happier with this, because as unfortunate as it is to say, I'm just nowhere near strong enough to overcome all of this. I have been trying and failing for years and I am exhausted of being the source of worry and pain for everyone around me. I just want to be at peace.


I wanted to do some good, because I felt that I had to do all I could. My projects, work, and anything else you might need is available free of charge for all my friends to use. Unfortunately, my discord account and probably the rest of my socials will be deleted in a year's time automatically, this website will be shut down soon too, probably in the first quarter of 2027 and will no longer be renewed, so please get anything you might need beforehand. I would have renewed it for more but I thought it would have been a waste, instead I donated all I had in my bank account between 3 charities: The Psoriasis Association, Cancer Research Institute, and UNICEF. I'm not saying this to make you feel like I'm a good person, but rather to encourage you to donate if you can spare the money.


But I suppose this is it, even though I did not want to write all of this, I thought it would not be right to leave having said nothing at all. I truly am sorry that it had to come to this.
